Research Experience
======
**Source apportionment of atmospheric ammonia in Taiyuan based on stable isotope tracing technology**

Shanxi Provincial Basic Research Program, 2023.09 - 2024.12

Main work:
1. Established a localized δ<sup>15</sup>N source profile for NH<sub>3</sub> emissions from the coking industry in Taiyuan.
2. Evaluated winter atmospheric ammonia isotope fractionation in Taiyuan based on synchronous observations.
3. Combined stable nitrogen isotopes to analyze the contributions of different emission sources to atmospheric ammonia.

**Source and spatiotemporal variation of atmospheric ammonia in Taiyuan based on nitrogen stable isotope analysis**

2024.06 - 2025.05

Main work:
1. Established localized δ<sup>15</sup>N source profiles for NH<sub>3</sub> emissions from livestock breeding, nitrogen fertilizer application, and traffic sources.
2. Conducted year-round observations of NH<sub>3</sub> concentration and stable nitrogen isotopes at five urban and suburban sites in Taiyuan.
3. Used stable isotope mixing models to quantify the spatiotemporal variation in source contributions to atmospheric NH<sub>3</sub>.
